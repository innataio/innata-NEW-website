OUR VISION

“Build the bridges for genetic condition patients to reach personalized treatments through Digitalization process.

Innovation Through Technology

Innata and its technology based solution applies Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to automate the collection of relevant data (i.e., medical records, DNA sequencing data, patient collected data), using high computer processing ( MS Azure), and advanced analytics to extract valuable data for researchers and clinicians to use in research and discovery findings. The innovation strategy is based on Data collection / AI Algorithm model. The information provided in our Innata Patients Centric Mobile Applications, their Medical records, included their Genetic Test results on phenotype/genotype variant is used by Machine Learning algorithms to create predictive models which will support the diagnosis process for patients with genetic based diseases.
